F.A.Q

  • What event services do we offer?

    We offer everything you need for your next event, including event design, event management, event marketing,event catering, event entertainment, and all the other myrid services that help make every event we plan a huge success.

  • Do you charge separately for your various event services?

    Some of our services come standard, others are priced separately. In order to get an idea of what your event will cost, contact us to speak with one of our friendly event planners today. They can give you a free estimate based on your individual needs.

  • Who handles my event?

    We have a whole team of event planners, event designer, and event management to meet you every need. They handle all the planning and arrangement. On the day of your event, they will be on hand to make sure everything runs smoothly so that you can focus on having a good time with your guests.

  • What is event design?

    Event design is the process of creating the perfect atmosphere for your event. It includes choosing the right decor, with elements like centerpieces,tables,chair, staging,linens,lounge furniture, and china. Great event design helps youe event stand out and leaves a lasting impression with everyone who attends.

  • What event management services do you provide?

    Our event management handle everything including arrivals and departures, ticketing and registration,securing the venue,permits,police and fire safety, security,transportation,valets,destination management,and anything else you can think of.

  • What are some event marketing ideas?

    We like to save our best event marketing ideas for when actually speak to you, but we do have several examples of ways to market your business/event.In the past we've put together creative invitations,custom gift bags, social media campaigns,and company logos on everything from wall projections to cakes.
